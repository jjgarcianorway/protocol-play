A dried riverbed stretching to the horizon. Cracked earth, abandoned boats tilted on their sides. In the distance: a city with smoke rising. In the foreground: a woman kneeling by the last puddle, testing the water. Military vehicles on the far bank. The sky is clear and blue — cruelly beautiful. Style: painterly, wide angle, photojournalistic composition. Colors: parched browns, dusty sky, cruel blue sky, military green. Mood: quiet desperation, the banality of catastrophe.
