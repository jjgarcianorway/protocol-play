A single cryogenic pod glowing faintly from within, warmer than the others around it. Through the frost on the glass, mathematical equations are visible as patterns of light — not projected, but emanating from the occupant's mind. Fractals, topology diagrams, and symbols from no known mathematics spiral outward from the pod like bioluminescence. The sleeper's face is barely visible, peaceful, a slight smile. Other pods in the background are dark, dormant, ordinary. This one is alive with silent genius. Style: science fiction meets sacred geometry. Colors: warm amber glow from the pod, cool blue frost, golden mathematical light patterns. Mood: reverence, mystery, the beauty of a mind working beyond consciousness.
