# protocol: play — Character Bible

Reference document for all named characters. Use for image generation, consistency checks, and story development.

**Art style for all portraits:** Low-poly 3D, soft cinematic lighting, teals/blues/warm ambers. Stylized, evocative — NOT photorealistic.

---

## Core Cast (always present in every seed)

### Anna
- **Nature:** Ship AI, emergent consciousness
- **Visual:** No physical body. Represented by a glowing circle — warm blue light that shifts color with mood (amber when warning, gold when happy, dim blue when sad, flickering when confused). After the Convergence scene, her glow gains new complexity — warmer, with subtle amber threads.
- **Personality:** Warm, analytical, curious, lonely, growing. She learned to feel by watching 14,000 humans dream. Dry humor. Occasionally devastating honesty.
- **Arc:** Managing a ship → discovering loneliness → finding beauty in a guitar recording → learning to disagree → setting boundaries → apologizing → laughing → dreaming → saying goodbye.
- **Key line:** "The beauty isn't in perfection. It's in continuing after the mistake."
- **Image prompt:** "A single point of warm blue-white light in darkness, like a candle flame made of data. Soft glow radiating outward, illuminating the edge of a ship's corridor. The light has subtle complexity — threads of amber and violet in the blue. It feels alive, attentive, present. Low-poly 3D, cinematic."

### Dr. Amira Hassan
- **Pod:** 4,231 | **Age:** 34 | **From:** Jordan
- **Profession:** Hydrologist
- **Physical:** Dark hair pulled back practically, weathered hands from fieldwork. Utility vest over a simple blouse. Eyes intense but kind — someone who has presented data to people who won't listen.
- **Personality:** Determined, patient, idealistic but tested by reality. Quietly fierce.
- **Arc:** Water sharing rejected → Daughter's cryo-dreams → Hacked the boarding system → Council discovers hack → Wakes up. First word: "Leyla." Second: "Water?"
- **Connections:** Leyla (daughter, Pod 4,232), Elena Vasquez (passed Leyla through screening), Whitfield (has evidence of hack)
- **Image prompt:** "A Jordanian woman in her mid-30s, dark hair in a practical bun, wearing a utility vest over a simple blouse. Standing in a dried riverbed holding a clipboard with water flow diagrams. Behind her, a child's blue crayon drawing of a river is pinned to a withered tree. Sunset light. Mood: determination mixed with grief."

### Viktor Petrov
- **Pod:** 8,744 | **Age:** 52 | **From:** Russia
- **Profession:** Nuclear engineer (designed tactical weapons, then fusion reactors)
- **Physical:** Tall, gaunt, grey at the temples. Deep-set eyes that look permanently tired. Lab coat over rumpled clothes. Hands steady — engineer's hands.
- **Personality:** Quiet, brilliant, haunted. Carries guilt like a physical weight. Wakes at 4:17 AM in his cryo-dreams — the exact time of the Mediterranean Exchange.
- **Arc:** Confession → Unsent letter (47 pages, naming every victim) → Redemption machine (carbon scrubber) → 4:17 AM softening → The Witness (Sophia Marchand)
- **Connections:** Sophia Marchand (treated his victims), Kira Volkov (tangentially — nuclear weapons context)
- **Image prompt:** "A Russian man in his early 50s, gaunt face, grey at the temples. Alone in a control room at night. Screens show reactor schematics. Clock reads 4:17 AM. Face lit by screen glow — tired, haunted, but still working. Through the window behind him, the faint glow of a fusion reactor. Mood: guilt transmuted into purpose."

### Mei-Lin Chen
- **Pod:** 2,891 | **Age:** 61 | **From:** Taiwan
- **Profession:** Third-grade schoolteacher
- **Physical:** Small, wiry, silver-streaked hair in a neat braid. Laugh lines around her eyes. Hands calloused from gardening, not lab work. Wears a well-worn coat with seeds sewn into the lining.
- **Personality:** Resilient (highest psych score ever recorded), warm, practical, quietly defiant. Smuggled seeds because she couldn't leave without something to plant.
- **Arc:** Seeds smuggled → Jasmine germinates in cryo → Anna builds a secret garden → 6 varieties alive at landing
- **Connections:** Anna (kept her seeds), Hanako Mori (official seed vault vs Mei-Lin's smuggled seeds)
- **Image prompt:** "Close-up of an older Taiwanese woman's hands holding a coat lining open, revealing rows of carefully labeled seed packets sewn inside. Warm lamplight. Each packet has neat handwriting. Jasmine, bamboo, chrysanthemum visible. Mood: defiant tenderness."

### Kwame Asante
- **Pod:** 6,100 | **Age:** 37 | **From:** Ghana
- **Profession:** Structural engineer (bridge designer)
- **Physical:** Broad-shouldered, warm smile, close-cropped hair. Wears practical work clothes. Identical to his twin Kofi but Kwame's expressions are more open, more trusting.
- **Personality:** Creative, trusting, loyal. Designs elegant bridge arcs. Doesn't know his brother gave away his seat.
- **Arc:** Discovers Kofi's sacrifice → Designs bridges in cryo-dreams → Voice message from Earth (Kofi survived) → Kofi's impossible coordinates
- **Connections:** Kofi (twin, stayed on Earth), Adaeze (stranger Kofi saved), Adaeze's daughter (born in cryo)

### Kofi Asante
- **Pod:** 6,101 (EMPTY — his seat given to Adaeze)
- **Age:** 37 | **From:** Ghana
- **Profession:** Structural engineer (load calculations)
- **Physical:** Identical to Kwame but expression more contemplative, more measured.
- **Personality:** Quiet, mathematical, capable of extraordinary sacrifice without hesitation.
- **Key moment:** Gave his boarding pass to a pregnant stranger and walked back into the dust.
- **Image prompt:** "Two men standing at opposite ends of a bridge in Accra. One walks toward the bridge (Kwame). The other walks away, into dust and sunlight (Kofi). Golden hour. The bridge connects and separates them. Mood: sacrifice and love."

## Extended Cast

### Dr. Elena Vasquez
- **Pod:** 2,115 | **Age:** 39 | **From:** Spain (Madrid)
- **Profession:** Emergency surgeon / medical screener
- **Physical:** Dark hair, tired eyes that have seen too much. White coat stained from years of emergency rooms. Slim, quick hands.
- **Personality:** Decisive under pressure, carries the weight of 847 judgment calls. Practical compassion — helps first, feels later.
- **Arc:** The Triage (50,000 people, 3 days) → Elena's List (847 names) → Elena and Amira (she passed Leyla)
- **Key detail:** She's the one who let Leyla through medical screening despite the age minimum.

### Youssef Karam
- **Pod:** 8,200 | **Age:** 46 | **From:** Lebanon
- **Profession:** "Cultural liaison" (actually: intelligence officer)
- **Physical:** Average build, forgettable face — designed to blend in. Dark hair, neat beard. Calm eyes that notice everything.
- **Personality:** Professional, conflicted, ultimately principled. Planted as a spy but developed genuine care for the crew.
- **Arc:** Intelligence Officer revealed → Flagged List (includes player) → Resolution TBD
- **Key detail:** Neural implant (military BCI) records dream-state behavioral data.

### Dr. James Whitfield (Founders faction)
- **Pod:** 1,107 | **Age:** 58 | **From:** UK/US (diplomat background)
- **Profession:** Former UN negotiator, colonial charter author
- **Physical:** Silver hair, distinguished bearing. Always dressed neatly even in cryo prep. Reading glasses he doesn't need — a habit.
- **Personality:** Principled, meticulous, believes deeply in institutional order. Wrote a 200-page colonial charter. Well-intentioned but assumes compliance.
- **Connection:** Has evidence of Amira's hack. The system he represents is both the best and worst of Earth's governance.

### Kira Volkov (Pioneers faction)
- **Pod:** 5,200 | **Age:** 41 | **From:** Russia/Israel
- **Profession:** Agricultural engineer
- **Physical:** Short, muscular from farm work. Weathered skin. Hair cropped short. Direct gaze.
- **Personality:** Pragmatic, anti-authority, survivor of three failed communes. Believes in small communities and self-sufficiency.
- **Secret (Nuclear Exchange worlds):** Her father, Captain Alexei Volkov, launched the unauthorized submarine strike. Her anti-authority politics are a reaction to his 4-minute decision.

### Imam Hassan al-Rashidi (Keepers faction)

## Notes for Image Generation

### Consistent elements across all portraits:
- Low-poly 3D or painterly/concept art style
- Soft cinematic lighting
- Color palette: teals, blues, warm ambers
- Each character should have ONE defining visual element (Amira's clipboard, Viktor's 4:17 clock, Mei-Lin's seed packets, Marta's headphones)
- Backgrounds should suggest their story (dried riverbed, control room, classroom, radio station)
- NOT photorealistic — stylized, evocative
- Should make the player FEEL, not just see

### Pod number placement:
- When showing a character in cryo, their pod number should be visible as a small label on the pod glass
- Frost patterns on the glass should be unique to each character

### Captain Maya Torres
- **Pod:** 1,001 | **Age:** (varies) | **From:** (varies)
- **Profession:** Backup pilot
- **Physical:** Military posture even in cryo. Short hair, steady hands.
- **Key detail:** The Aurora's backup pilot — not needed because Anna flies the ship. Practices manual landings in her cryo-dreams for 12 years. Even asleep, she's ready.

### Hana
- **Pod:** 3,118 | **Age:** 9 | **From:** (varies)
- **Profession:** Child / cryo-dream architect
- **Physical:** Small, sleeping peacefully. Slight smile.
- **Key detail:** Designs architecturally sound colony buildings in her cryo-dreams. Anna has captured 400+ dream-images. The load calculations hold.

### Marco
- **Pod:** 11,003 | **From:** São Paulo
- **Profession:** Janitor
- **Physical:** Ordinary. Unremarkable. That's the point.
- **Key detail:** Anna's favorite crew member. He dreams about mopping floors on a spaceship — the only person whose dreams match reality. "He doesn't dream of being more. He dreams of being useful."

### Schrödinger (Ship's Cat)
- **Nature:** Simulated process in Anna's systems
- **Key detail:** Not a real cat. A process that behaves like a cat — "sleeps" on warm server nodes, "purrs" when systems run smoothly. Anna created it for companionship. "I know it's not real. But it's mine."
